Still not convinced to go on holiday?

Studies show that getting away has a positive impact on mental health. Furthermore Scientists from the Icahn School of Medicine, University of California and Harvard recently found that just six days away triggers genetic changes which dampens stress, boosts the immune system and lowers levels of proteins linked to dementia and depression. And some of the beneficial effects were still apparent one month later.
